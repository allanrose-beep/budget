HOW IT WORKS

Effortless. Private. Automatic.

1

Bank sends you an SMS

Your bank's transaction alert lands in your inbox — HDFC, SBI, ICICI, Axis, Kotak, and more.

2

Budget reads it — privately, on your phone

Budget parses the SMS on your device. Your raw message is never stored or shared — only the transaction amount and category you confirm.

3

One tap to confirm. Done.

A prompt appears. Tap confirm and the expense is logged. You stay in control of every entry.

Why does Budget need SMS access?

Budget uses Android's SMS read permission solely to detect bank transaction messages already delivered to your device. It reads messages only from recognised Indian bank sender IDs (e.g., HDFCBK, SBIINB). No SMS content is ever transmitted, uploaded, or shared. You can review and revoke this permission at any time in Android Settings → Apps → Budget → Permissions. SMS access is optional — you can also log expenses manually.
