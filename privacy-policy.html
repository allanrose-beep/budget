Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-29 · Effective immediately

1. Who We Are

Allan Rose (Product Manager) built the Budget app as a free personal finance tool. This Privacy Policy explains what data Budget accesses, how it is used and stored, and your rights.

Contact: pmallan.rose@gmail.com

2. Data Budget Accesses and Stores

Your Unique Account ID

When you first open Budget, the app automatically generates a unique encrypted ID for your account. This ID has no connection to your name, email address, phone number, or any personal identifier. You are never asked to register or log in — the ID is created silently and exists only to keep your data separate from everyone else's. Nobody outside your device knows what this ID maps to. This is handled via Firebase Authentication (a Google service).

Transaction Data

Expense records you confirm — amount, category, and date — are stored in a secure, encrypted private cloud vault linked to your unique ID. No personal information is attached to these records. If you delete an expense inside the app, it is permanently deleted from the vault immediately. If you delete your account, the vault and your unique ID are permanently erased. This storage is provided by Google Firebase Firestore, encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.

SMS Messages

Budget requests Android's READ_SMS permission to detect bank transaction messages already delivered to your device. Budget reads only from recognised Indian bank sender IDs (e.g., HDFCBK, SBIINB, ICICIB, AXISBK, KOTAKB). The raw SMS text is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted or stored anywhere. Only the transaction details you explicitly confirm are saved.

Notifications (UPI apps)

Budget uses Android's NotificationListenerService to read payment notifications from UPI apps (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm). Notification content is processed on-device only. As with SMS, only the transaction details you confirm are saved — the raw notification text is discarded.

App Preferences & Budget Settings

Budget limits, category preferences, and notification settings are stored both locally on your device and in your Firebase account, so your settings are preserved if you reinstall the app.

3. Cloud Infrastructure

Budget's secure cloud vault is built on Google Firebase — the same infrastructure used by Google Pay and other trusted Google services. Your data is stored in a private, encrypted environment. Google acts as a data processor on our behalf under Google's data processing terms and does not use your data for advertising. The unique ID that identifies your vault is not linked to your real-world identity in any Google system. For more information: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4. What Budget Does NOT Do

  • Does not link your financial data to your real-world identity.
  • Does not store raw SMS content — only confirmed transaction amounts and categories.
  • Does not sell, share, or disclose your data to advertisers or third parties.
  • Does not access your bank account directly.
  • Does not use advertising SDKs or analytics SDKs that track you across apps.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your transaction data is stored in a Google Firebase account identified only by an anonymous user ID — with no personal identifiers attached. Data is encrypted in transit using TLS and encrypted at rest by Firebase. Budget settings and preferences are also stored locally on your device for offline access.

Your Firebase account and all associated data can be permanently deleted from within the app at any time via Settings → Delete My Data. Uninstalling the app removes local data but does not automatically delete your Firebase data — use the in-app deletion option for complete removal.

6. Your Rights & Account Deletion

You can delete all your data — including your anonymous Firebase account and every expense record linked to it — directly from the app via Settings → Delete My Data. This is instant and permanent.

If you have already uninstalled the app, submit a deletion request using the form below. We will process your request within 7 days and send a confirmation to the email you provide.

→ Request Account Deletion (web form)

7. Monetisation

Budget is currently free to use with no advertisements and no in-app purchases. We may introduce optional paid features or other monetisation in the future. If we do, this policy and the app store listing will be updated to reflect those changes before they take effect.

8. Children's Privacy

Budget is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
